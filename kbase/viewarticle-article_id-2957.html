Cooking - Pies
Introduction | The Pies


Introduction

There are a variety of pies available to cook in RuneScape, from the ever-so-simple redberry pie (beloved by Imcando dwarves, erm, everywhere), to the notoriously difficult summer pie. The table below shows the ingredients in the order that you should add them to the pie shell.

For more information about making a pie shell, see Cooking - Cooking Methods. For more information about how much pies heal, please refer to the Members' and Non-Members' tables.

The Pies

All pies must be baked on a range before they can be eaten (or wielded, in the case of the mud pie), and the experience listed is for the complete process.
