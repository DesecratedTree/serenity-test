Payment Comparison

Use our payment comparison table to find the most suitable payment method for purchasing your chosen membership. You can find detailed information about a particular payment method by clicking 'More Information'.

The first "Cost per month" column refers to purchasing a RuneScape subscription; the second refers to purchasing a combined discounted subscription for both RuneScape and FunOrb.

Please note that if you began your subscription before the 18th of August 2008, you will continue to pay at the lower cost (USD$5.00 / GBP£3.20 if paying by credit/debit card). If your subscription lapses, you will be able to resubscribe and continue paying this lower price for a period of two weeks. If you pay at the higher rate for 12 months (without a gap of more than 14 days) you will be offered the lower, $5 (£3.20) rate at the end of that year.

Billing Partners

Jagex Ltd and RuneScape are dedicated to using trusted billing partners that are carefully screened according to strict industry standards. These companies do not retain, share, store or use your personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.

WorldPay


WorldPay is an important part of The Royal Bank of Scotland Group, the 5th largest banking group in the world, and is used by thousands of businesses worldwide.

WorldPay is committed to protecting the security of your data. Their payment system uses a variety of security processes to ensure the safety of your personal information.

PayByCash


PayByCash has been a major online payment systems provider to the computer games industry since 1998. They provide various payment options and have a dedicated customer support team who will answer any questions you may have.

They take your security and privacy very seriously and employ state-of-the-art data encryption to ensure safe and secure transactions.

Surfpin


Surfpin allows players to instantly and securely purchase RuneScape membership via their mobile phone (PayBySMS) and their landline (PayByPhone).

PayBySMS is currently available in Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Ireland, the Netherlands, Norway, Sweden, the United Kingdom and the United States. PayByPhone is available in the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Belgium, Switzerland, the Netherlands and Germany.

Customer satisfaction is of extreme importance to Surfpin and their experienced support team are happy to answer any queries that you may have.

Surfpin offer PayByPhone support and PayBySMS support. Both pages also include answers to many frequently asked questions.

PayPal


PayPal is a safe, easy way to pay online. The service allows anyone to pay in any way they prefer, including through credit cards, bank accounts, buyer credit or account balances, without sharing financial information.

PayPal treats your information with the utmost care, and, in addition to highest quality encryption and other security measures, works closely with a number of law enforcement agencies to prevent fraud and identity theft.
